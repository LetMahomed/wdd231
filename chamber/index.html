Events

Cabo Frio hosts a variety of vibrant events throughout the year, celebrating its rich culture and stunning natural beauty. One of the most popular is the Cabo Frio Carnival, where colorful parades, samba music, and street parties fill the city with excitement. The Festa de São Benedito in January showcases local traditions, featuring religious processions and cultural performances. Additionally, the Cabo Frio Jazz Festival attracts music lovers with performances by renowned artists in a scenic beach setting. The annual Cabo Frio International Film Festival also brings together filmmakers and cinema enthusiasts, showcasing a diverse selection of films. Each event offers a unique glimpse into the local culture and the lively spirit of this beautiful coastal city.
