Site Purpose

Surf Scout is designed to help surfers and outdoor adventurers find the best locations and current conditions for their next trip. The site provides real-time wave data, weather updates, tide information, and an interactive map of surf spots and hiking areas. Whether you're chasing swells or planning a scenic coastal hike, Surf Scout makes it easy to plan with confidence.
