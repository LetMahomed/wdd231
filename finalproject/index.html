Site Purpose

Surf Scout is designed to help surfers and outdoor adventurers find the best locations and current conditions for their next trip. The site provides real-time wave data, weather updates, tide information, and an interactive map of surf spots and hiking areas.

Color Scheme

Primary Color: Blue (#0077be) — used in the header, footer, and as a navigation accent to represent water and trust.

Secondary Color: Beige (#f4e1c1) — used as the background color for the body to create a calm, sandy beach-like tone.

Typography

Heading Font: Poppins — used for all headers (h1, h2, h3) for a bold, modern look.

Body Font: Roboto — used for paragraphs and general text for high readability and clean appearance.
